About Facts & Fakes

Facts & Fakes is an AI-powered news fact-checking platform built to address a fundamental gap in how we evaluate news: the difference between how something is reported and whether it's actually true.

Our two-layer analysis system gives you both answers at once — making it the only fact-checker that separates the accuracy of the journalism from the accuracy of the underlying claims.

Built with a swarm of AI systems working in consensus: Claude, GPT-4, DeepSeek, and Cohere. No single AI makes the verdict — they debate it.
